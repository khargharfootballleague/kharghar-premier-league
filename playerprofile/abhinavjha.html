The Versatile Finisher

Abhinav Jha is the definition of a dual-threat defender. While many defender focus purely on defense, Abhinav’s Season 3 campaign was a testament to his all-round game, recording **7 goals and 4 assists**. He is a "big moment" player. His spectacular equalizer against Kharghar United remains one of the highlight goals of the season—a powerful shot from range that showcased his clinical precision and raw shooting power.
